Academic Information

This course is taught at the Instituto de Investigación en Comunicación Óptica (IICO) of the Universidad Autónoma de San Luis Potosí.

The Modern Physics course covers fundamental concepts including quantum mechanics, relativity, atomic and nuclear physics, providing students with essential knowledge for advanced studies in physics and related fields.
