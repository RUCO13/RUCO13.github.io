About me

  • I hold a PhD in Photonics from IICO-UASLP and currently work as a Physics professor at Universidad Politecnica de San Luis Potosi

  • I am passionate about exploring the field of Solid State Experimental Physics and Computational Calculation of Semiconductor Bandstructures. My current focus is on expanding my knowledge in DFT (Density Functional Theory) and band structure packages.

  • I take pride in being the founder of NanophotonIICOs, an institutional repository dedicated to advancing research in the field of nanophotonics.

  • Feel free to ask me anything related to my areas of expertise or discuss potential collaborations.
